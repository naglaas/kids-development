Main Types of Clouds

Clouds are made up of very light water droplets or ice crystals. Watching the clouds is not only fun, but it can give you clues about what kinds of weather are coming.
There are many kinds of Clouds you can watch in the sky. Here are the three main types of the clouds

Cirrus Clouds

Thin, wispy clouds. They are high in the sky where it is very cold. They appear in the fine weather days. By watching the movement of cirrus clouds you can tell from which direction weather is approaching. When you see cirrus clouds, it usually indicates that a change in the weather will occur within 24 hours.

Stratus Clouds

Flat clouds. They are often gray and dull. They hide the sun and they often bring rain.They often cover the whole sky.

Cumulus Clouds

Bid, white, and fluffy clouds that look like pieces of floating cotton. They are seen on the warm and sunny days.These Clouds grow upward and they can develop into thunderstorm clouds.
